What is this?

This is a url shortener for prechacthis.org.

Examples

  • http://prech.ac/2.5p3.5p4.5p
  • http://prech.ac/3p3p3p1
  • http://prech.ac/332p2

Patterns are assumed to be two people unless otherwise specified, and may be specified by adding the number of people and a colon (:) as a prefix to the pattern. Examples:

  • http://prech.ac/1:534
  • http://prech.ac/2:2.5p2.5p1
  • http://prech.ac/3:3p4p334p3
